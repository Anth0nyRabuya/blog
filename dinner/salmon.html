Baked Salmon with Lemon

4.9 ⭐ | 900 Reviews

This baked salmon with lemon is simple, healthy, and packed with flavor! A perfect light meal for any day of the week.

"Absolutely delicious! The lemon adds a refreshing flavor that complements the salmon perfectly."

Samantha

"This is my go-to recipe for a healthy dinner. The salmon turns out so tender and juicy every time."

David

Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
Instructions:

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or aluminum foil.

Step 2: Place the salmon fillets on the baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and rub to coat evenly.

Step 3: Sprinkle minced garlic, thyme, rosemary, salt, and pepper over the salmon fillets.

Step 4: Top each fillet with lemon slices.

Step 5: Bake the salmon for 12-15 minutes, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.

Step 6: Remove from the oven and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
