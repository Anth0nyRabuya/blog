The Best Swedish Meatballs

Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup minced onion
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 tsp allspice
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
Instructions:

Step 1: In a large bowl, combine ground beef, ground pork, breadcrumbs, minced onion, egg, allspice, nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Mix until just combined.

Step 2: Shape the mixture into small meatballs (about 1 inch in diameter). Heat a skillet over medium heat, then add butter and brown the meatballs on all sides. Remove and set aside.

Step 3: In the same skillet, whisk in flour and cook for 1 minute. Gradually add beef broth and heavy cream, whisking until smooth.

Step 4: Return the meatballs to the skillet and simmer for 10-15 minutes until cooked through and the gravy thickens. Serve warm.
