Caprese Salad with Balsamic Glaze

4.9 ⭐ | 850 Reviews

A delicious Caprese salad with ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, basil, and a sweet balsamic glaze drizzle.

"This salad was fantastic—so fresh and full of flavor, especially with the balsamic glaze!"

Anna

"Absolutely love the combination of mozzarella and tomatoes with the balsamic glaze. A perfect appetizer!"

Jake

Recipe

Ingredients:
  • 4 ripe tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:

Step 1: Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a large platter, alternating them and layering with basil leaves.

Step 2: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper.

Step 3: Drizzle the balsamic glaze over the salad and serve immediately.
